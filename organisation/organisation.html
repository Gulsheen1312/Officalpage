Classes during covid

We have moved all French courses online for the duration of the current crisis.

With an extraordinary panel of trained, certified teachers from the Ministry of Education, France, and our online interactive methods, now you can have a tutor right in your living room!

Don’t let the coronavirus stop you from learning French. Enroll in one of our courses.

New students with prior knowledge of French will be given a short assessment of their current level via orientation test to ensure they are assigned to an appropriate group. Assessments are done by Evalang & video interview via Zoom/WhatsApp usually. This will be arranged once you apply for an orientation test.

For complete beginners, there is no need for an assessment. You start at the beginning!

About

Founder of GFC - Gulsheen Kaur

Aim of GFC school of languages

GFC GULSHEEN FRENCH CLASSES is an organization that aims to promote the French language and francophone culture around the world. French: For Art and Aesthetics French is one of the leading languages of Europe and the world. French is an official language of many of the world’s organizations such as the Untied Nations, Nato, UNESCO, the International Red Cross Association, and numerous other international committees and organizations. After English, French is one of the most learned languages around the globe. French is a great step towards building a world-class education that can open many doors in employment in a variety of occupations such as teaching, interpretation, translation, travel and tourism, and many more. French is spoken in five continents, making French one of the most useful second language for traveling
